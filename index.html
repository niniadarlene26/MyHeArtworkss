About Me

Hellooo! I'm Ninia💜. Drawing is one of my favorite hobbies because it helps me relax and express my creativity through art. I feel happy and motivated whenever I notice that my skills are improving.

I have been interested in drawing since I was around five years old. My interest in art began when I discovered anime. I was fascinated by the different character designs, so I started drawing anime eyes and the faces of various anime characters. As time went by, I became more passionate about drawing, and it eventually became one of my favorite hobbies.

For me, drawing is not just about creating artworks. It is also a way to express my imagination, creativity, and emotions. I enjoy challenging myself to learn new technique and explore different art styles while seeing how much my skills improve over time. Even though I still have a lot to learn, I am excited to continue my artistic journey and create more artworks in the future.
